Instant Noodles

Description

It's instant noodles. If you can't cook this you need help.

Ingredients

  • Instant Noodles
  • Some Green Vegetable
  • An Egg
  • Some Protein

Steps

  1. Boil water, add protein into the water if it is not instant protein or already cooked
  2. After water boils, add noodles and seasoning, and egg
  3. Let the food cook for 3 minutes, gentling seperating the noodles to cook evenly
  4. Add in the vegetables and cook another 30 seconds, then serve
